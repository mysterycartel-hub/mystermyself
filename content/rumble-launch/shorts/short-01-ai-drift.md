## Script

I looked away for three sessions and my AI invented a whole new brand.

New colors. New characters. A name I never approved. It forgot my avatar. It forgot my handle. It made up a logo that doesn't exist.

That's AI drift. And it happens fast.

You think you're building. You think it remembers. But every session starts from zero. It doesn't know what you locked in last week. It doesn't know your canon. It just... guesses.

Three days of work. Gone. Because I trusted memory that wasn't there.

So I fixed it. I wrote down everything. Brand colors. Character names. Locked rules. I gave the AI a source of truth it can't ignore.

Now every session starts with my brand — not its imagination.

Follow @mysterycartel. I'll show you exactly how.
