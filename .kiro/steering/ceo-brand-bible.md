## Brand Defaults

- **Colors:** Black / Gold / Red / Green / Fire Orange
- **Trading Chef Avatar:** Black male, chef hat, apron, gold chain, glasses, confident, urban cartoon style
- **Food Brand Slogan:** "Feeding the World, One Wing at a Time."
- **Payment CTAs:** Cash App, PayPal, Zelle, Stan Store
- **Default Funnel:** Free hook → email/Telegram capture → $27-47 ebook → high-ticket system

- **Brand Truth:** "I had the vision for years. I just had to start."
- **F1 Outro:** Every Trading Chef piece ends with — Chef's Golden Rule: [trading wisdom in kitchen metaphor]
