## Script

I tried every AI model out there. Every single one.

And look — they're all impressive in their own way. They can write. They can summarize. They can spit out code. But none of them understood what I was actually building.

I needed something that could hold a brand voice across fifty conversations. Something that didn't reset on me. Something that could write content that sounds like a real person said it — not a press release from a Fortune 500 company nobody cares about.

Claude got it. First try.

I gave it the brand. I gave it the lingo. I gave it the vision. And it didn't just repeat it back — it built on it. It became the strategy brain behind this whole operation. The scripts, the systems, the content architecture — all of it runs through Claude now.

This isn't an ad. Nobody's paying me. I switched because it worked. And @mysterycartel exists the way it does because of that decision.

That's the real.
